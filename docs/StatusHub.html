StatusHub

StatusHub is a flexible IT incident, status, and event communication tool helping to keep your impacted staff and customers informed effectively during service disruptions. To integrate StatusHub with Zenduty, complete the following steps:

In Zenduty:

  1. To add a new StatusHub integration, go to "Teams" on Zenduty and click on the "Manage" button corresponding to the team you want to add the integration to.

  2. Next, go to "Services" and click on the "Manage" button correspoding to the relevant Service.

  3. Go to "Integrations" and then "Add New Integration". Give it a name and select the application "StatusHub" from the dropdown menu.

  4. Go to "Configure" under your integrations and copy the webhooks URL generated.

In StatusHub:

  1. Click on Create a Status site. Enter the Status site name and Status site subdomain. Under Services, enter the Group name and service names. Click on Create Status site.

  1. Go To Dashboard. Click on the newly created Site (here Zenduty Site) to view the status of the site and services.

  1. Click Subscribe on the top right of the screen.

  1. Click on Webhook, paste the link copied earlier and click on Subscribe.

  1. Go to Dashboard, click on Settings. Select API Key and click on generate API key.

  1. Go back to Dashboard, click on "Platform Alerts" and enable.

  1. Enter the alert title and alert message and save it.

  2. Click on "add incident" in the dashboard and enter the title and message.

Select "Service Disruption" for "Website Application" and then "Create Incident".

  1. Click Zenduty site in the dashboard to view the status of site and services.

  1. StatusHub is now integrated.
